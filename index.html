ABOUT

I founded and ran a digital product studio called Self Titled for 3 years and delivered product and marketing leadership for high-growth startups and new initiatives in mid-market companies. My focus has been around building strong, innovative, business-oriented teams, leading research efforts to uncover market opportunities, and leading product management and product design efforts. Prior to that I spent 7 years working my way around the San Francisco tech community building everything from ecommerce sites, mobile apps, marketing programs, and enterprise software. I approach every project from a systems-thinking perspective and I believe that, when done right, systems give us an opportunity to scale knowledge.

I take leadership pretty seriously and have learned ton over the years (primarily from my failures). take it so seriously because I have seen the outcome when teams are led well, and also when they are not led well. I speak frequently on the topic and spent much of my time at Self Titled helping startups and other rapidly scaling companies train up leadership teams and build systems (told you I'm a systems guy) to bring out the best of teams.
